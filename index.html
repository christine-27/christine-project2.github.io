Hu Yitian is a Chinese actor. He made his acting debut in the television drama A Rush to Dead Summer, and subsequently starred in the 2017 hit web series A Love So Beautiful which brought him wider recognition and several newcomer awards.

Hu Yi Tian is basking in the limelight after breakout drama

Hu Yi Tian was virtually unknown when A Love So Beautiful started airing back in November but the drama which served as his first starring role quickly grew a cult following. It's only been two months since then and the 24-year-old actor has been gaining exposure from frequent TV appearances to magazine features (he's in InStyle, L'Officiel and Harper's Bazaar). The concept in his latest photoshoot seems quite fitting in that sense, and I imagine a bright future ahead for the newbie actor. I haven't really seen A Love So Beautiful yet, but I hear good things.

A Celebration Of Love: 5 C-Dramas And TW-Dramas

It’s been another whole year, and Valentine’s Day is dawning upon us once again. For some of us, this might mean a huge eye roll, while for others, it might be the perfect excuse to be a little extra with displays of affection toward significant others.
